Measure force:

Calculate passive force by resting your finger on a scale. Do not apply any pressure. Measure the mass in grams.

Calculate active force by pressing your finger hard on the scale. Apply as much pressure as you can comfortably manage. Measure the mass in grams.

The scale edge should be in-line with the dot on your finger base.
